About

I hold a Bachelor of Laws (LL.B.) and built my professional foundation within the Brazilian judicial system, where I worked for over five years supporting hearings, drafting legal documents and handling sensitive information in high-responsibility environments.

Working in courts shaped how I understand decision-making. Documentation is not administrative. It is evidence. It defines what can be verified, defended and trusted.

After moving to the United Kingdom, I expanded my experience into corporate environments, including roles supporting operations, stakeholders and administrative processes in regulated settings such as Network Rail and CBRE.

Alongside my professional experience, I have been developing knowledge in governance, risk, compliance and AI (Artificial Intelligence), focusing on how structured processes and accountability frameworks support decision-making in complex systems.

My work sits at the intersection of governance, documentation, risk and intelligent systems. I am particularly interested in roles where structure, accuracy and accountability are essential.
